About the Study

This research is looking into ways of moving away from centralised mainframe solutions of data analysing and decision making, towards citizen-led collective sensemaking. We are conducting a study into sharing of personal tracker data between occupants in workplaces, and trialling this prototype of an online system for it. This system gives people more ways to explore their data, enabling them to share their data with friends and colleagues and discuss it together.
